- **Music Rooms** - Real-time listening parties where friends can join and chat

- **Anonymous Listening** - Option to hide what you're currently playing
- **Music Recommendations Engine** - AI-powered suggestions based on listening patterns

- **Radio Stations** - Create custom radio stations based on artists/genres
- **Music Quiz Games** - Guess the song, artist, or year
- **Concert Notifications** - Alert users when their favorite artists are touring nearby

- **Dark/Light Theme Toggle** - Multiple UI themes
